Bug Fixes:
- Fixed Huskar boss burning spear not working 
- Fixed the blind on Hellfire Lantern debuff not stacking
- Fixed ability sounds not playing for Lyralei and Sacred Warrior

Bosses:
- Bosses are now considered as immovable objects 
- All Bosses magic immunity decreased to 25% (including mid boss)
- Boss AI has been improved
  - Changing attack target is now much smoother than before (before it would have a slight delay before attacking the new target)
  - Will change attack target if the current target becomes invulnerable or untargetable in any way (e.g. manta phasing, astral prison, etc.)
  - Will no longer keep chasing the attack target if they run out of the attack zone
  - Attack targets are now chosen based on how close they are to the boss, meaning it will target illusions, units, etc, instead of always focusing your hero
  - Will no longer cast abilities as soon as target becomes visible, but rather has a small delay before casting now

Windranger:
- Focus Fire cooldown decreased from 20 15 10 > 10 8 6

Map:
- Changed the middle zone area to reflect more on the new boss
- Improved map layout to be less empty
- Moved Night Stalker closer to the rune
- Added navmesh block near boss shrines so you don't get stuck teleporting
- Fixed navmesh for wards around some boss areas being too big
- Reworked duel area

Gameplay:
- Drakes are no longer considered as ancients and can be dominated
- Added 2 new abilities to every creep type
- Creep camps levels now scale with their initial spawn time instead of every 60 seconds
- Added new camp past ancients: "immortal camp" with units that level once every 10 minutes with max level 10 (can be dominated)
- Improved experience needed per level

Items:
Mirror Blade:
- Illusion created now belongs to the caster instead of the fountain (makes it work in chronosphere) but can't be controlled by any means.

- Rewind cooldown decreased decreased from 1 2 3 > 3
- Rewind can no longer lower any cooldown to any value below 1 second
